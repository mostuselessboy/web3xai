WebxAI Summer 2023, a global event spanning three weekends, brought together over 10,000+ enthusiasts of Web 3.0, blockchain, and artificial intelligence. The program featured critical analyses of blockchain, funding discussions, and the convergence of AI. Through founders' talks, growth discussions, and deep dives into Web 3.0, the event facilitated the integration of knowledge and creativity among professionals. The speaker lineup included CEOs and founders from leading companies like Rariko, StackOS, Foundership, Web 3 Accelerator, Zomato, and host Anurag Manik, creating a dynamic synergy between the cutting-edge realms of Web 3.0 and artificial intelligence.
